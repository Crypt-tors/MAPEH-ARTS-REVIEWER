PAINTINGS FROM ANCIENT EGYPT

• The purpose of Egyptian paintings is to make the deceased afterlife place pleasant.

• Their paintings emphasizes the importance of life after death and the preservation of the
knowledge of the past

• Their paintings were highly stylized,symbolic and shows profile view of an animal
or a person. The main colors used were red,black,blue,gold and green derived
from mineral pigments that can withstand strong sunlight without fading.

• Egyptian paintings are called HIREGLYPICS.(the most well-known types of drawings.)

COMMON METHODS OF GREEK PAINTING

• FRESCO - method of painting,water- based pigments on a freshly applied
plaster usually on a wall surface.
- Colors are made of ground powderpigments mixed in pure water
dried and set with plaster to become a permanents part of the wall,Ideal for
murals,durable and has a matte style.

- A fresco is a type of wall painting. The term comes from the italian word
for fresh because plaster is applied to the walls while still wet.

PANEL PAINTING

• Paintings on flat panels of wood

• It can be small,single-pieced or several panels joined together.

• Most of the panel paintings now no longer exist because of their organic composition.
